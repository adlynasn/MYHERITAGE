FAQs

The mission of MYHeritage is to promote and preserve Malaysian heritage by showcasing the traditional crafts and skills of local artisans. We aim to provide a platform where these artisans can share their craft with the world and continue their cultural traditions.

MYHeritage is based in Malaysia, and we are proud to be a part of the vibrant and diverse Malaysian community.

At MYHeritage, we carefully select artisans and products based on their authenticity, quality, and cultural significance. We collaborate with artisans who are skilled in traditional Malaysian crafts and are committed to preserving their heritage.

MYHeritage contributes to the local community by supporting local artisans, providing them with a platform to showcase their talents, and creating opportunities for economic empowerment. We also aim to raise awareness about Malaysian heritage and culture through our products and storytelling
